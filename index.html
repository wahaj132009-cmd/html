Saim Ayub

Saim Ayub (Urdu: صائم ایوب; born 24 May 2002) is a Pakistani international cricketer.He plays for the Pakistan national team as an all-rounder, who bats left-handed and bowls right-arm off break. He represents Karachi Whites in domestic cricket and Peshawar Zalmi in the Pakistan Super League.Early life His father Ayub Ahmed and uncle Mohammad Javed both played cricket at the club level. Saim was initiated into the game at a young age with tape ball cricket from Gulistan-e-Johar, helped by his father as well as by two elder brothers. Domestic and franchise career In February 2021, Saim made his Twenty20 debut for the Quetta Gladiators in the 2021 Pakistan Super League. In March 2022, he made his List A debut for Sindh in the 2021–22 Pakistan Cup. In September 2022, he made his first-class debut for Sindh in the 2022–23 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy.
